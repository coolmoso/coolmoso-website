About CoolMoso

Founded in China, CoolMoso is a beloved lifestyle brand specializing in premium plush toys and comfort products. Our name combines 'Liv' (living) and 'Heart' — representing our mission to bring warmth and happiness into everyday life.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to creating the softest, most huggable companions that bring joy to people of all ages. Every CoolMoso product is designed with meticulous attention to detail, using premium materials that are gentle to the touch.

Our Products

Each CoolMoso creation is crafted with love and precision, combining Chinese design sensibility with the highest quality materials. From the iconic Nemu Nemu series to our Forest Roommate characters, every piece tells a story of warmth and companionship.

Our Journey

2005

Brand Founded

CoolMoso was established in China with a vision to create the world's softest plush companions.

2010

Nemu Nemu Series Launch

Our iconic sleeping animal series launched, quickly becoming a fan favorite across China.

2015

International Expansion

CoolMoso expanded to China and Southeast Asia, bringing comfort to millions more.

2020

Forest Roommate Series

The beloved Forest Roommate character series debuted, featuring adorable woodland creatures.

2024

Innovation Continues

Launched new eco-friendly materials and expanded our character universe with exciting new IPs.
