HAM Hamster Series Cute Launch!

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of our brand-new HAM Hamster Series — the cutest addition to the CoolMoso family yet! Inspired by the endearing charm of real hamsters, every plush in this collection captures those signature chubby cheeks, tiny paws, and bright, curious eyes that make you want to squeeze them all day long.

Each HAM Hamster is crafted from our premium ultra-soft short-pile fabric, making them incredibly huggable while remaining gentle on skin. The collection features five distinct hamster characters, each with their own unique colour palette and personality — from the rosy-cheeked Cotton Candy Ham to the sleepy Midnight Bun curled up in a cozy little ball.

The HAM series is available in three sizes: Mini (15 cm), Standard (25 cm), and Jumbo (40 cm), so there is a perfect companion for every shelf, desk, or cuddle session. Special limited-edition gift sets are also available, paired with a collectible mini sticker sheet and a hand-written character profile card.

Whether you are a lifelong plush collector or simply looking for the perfect heartfelt gift, the HAM Hamster Series is sure to bring warmth and smiles. Visit your nearest CoolMoso store or check our online channels to get yours before they sell out!
